Privacy Policy

Ammar & Co (UK) Ltd
Last updated: 22 April 2026

Ammar & Co (UK) Ltd respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use and protect personal information when you visit this website, contact us, or send us an enquiry.

1. Who we are

Ammar & Co (UK) Ltd is a property development company operating in the United Kingdom.

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or about how we use your personal data, you can contact us at:

  • Email: info@ammarltd.co.uk
  • Land enquiries: land@ammarltd.co.uk
  • Sales enquiries: sales@ammarltd.co.uk
  • Accounts enquiries: accounts@ammarltd.co.uk

2. What information we may collect

We may collect and process the following personal data:

  • your name
  • your email address
  • your phone number
  • details you provide in an enquiry
  • property or land details you send to us
  • technical website usage information such as browser type, device type and pages visited

3. How we collect your data

We may collect your data when:

  • you email us directly
  • you click a contact link on this website
  • you submit information about land, property or business enquiries
  • you browse this website

4. How we use your data

We may use your personal data to:

  • respond to enquiries
  • assess land or property opportunities
  • communicate about developments, projects or sales matters
  • manage business relationships
  • improve our website and user experience
  • keep appropriate internal records
  • comply with legal or regulatory obligations

5. Legal basis for processing

We process personal data where necessary for one or more of the following reasons:

  • to respond to your request before entering into a potential business arrangement
  • for our legitimate interests in operating and improving our business
  • to comply with legal obligations
  • where you have provided consent, where consent is required

6. How long we keep your data

We keep personal data only for as long as reasonably necessary for the purpose it was collected, including to deal with enquiries, maintain records, and comply with legal, tax or regulatory requirements.

7. Sharing your data

We do not sell your personal data.

We may share your data where necessary with professional advisers or service providers, such as:

  • solicitors
  • accountants
  • estate agents
  • consultants
  • website or email service providers

We may also disclose personal data where required by law or where necessary to protect our legal rights.

8. Cookies and website analytics

This website may use basic cookies or similar technologies to help the website function and to understand general usage.

If analytics or additional tracking tools are added in future, this Privacy Policy may be updated accordingly.

9. Data security

We take reasonable steps to protect personal data against unauthorised access, loss, misuse or disclosure. However, no method of internet transmission or storage is completely secure.

10. Your rights

Under UK data protection law, you may have rights including the right to:

  • request access to your personal data
  • request correction of inaccurate data
  • request deletion of your data in certain circumstances
  • object to certain processing
  • request restriction of processing in certain circumstances
  • lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner’s Office (ICO)
